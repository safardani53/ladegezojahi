Jared Goff Mother, Nancy Goff [2023 Update]

An exemplary mom & wife, Nancy Goff is the mother of NFL player Jared Goff. While her husband Jerry mentored their son Jared to football prominence, she stood by his side as his biggest supporter.

Nancy is a UC Berkeley alumna who works at CrossCountry Mortgage, LLC. Besides her professional career, she is happy to be the proud mother of two-time Pro Bowl Jared Goff.

Goff became the first overall draft of the Los Angeles Rams in the 2016 NFL Draft and played for 6 seasons.

A four-year deal with the Rams in 2019 before a trade to Detriot Lions in 2021 sees him on contract through the 2023-24 season.

Jared Goff led the Rams to two NFC West division victories, three playoff appearances, and Super Bowl LIII.

Many are already aware of Jared’s career achievements, but the details of his family remain quite in the shadows. Here in this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about his mother, Nancy Goff.

Jared Goff’s Mother | Who Is Nancy Goff?

Nancy Ann Goff is a native of Marin County, California. She was born in 1965, but her exact birthday is not known. Mrs. Goff is currently in her late 50s.

She is the mother of two kids: son Jared Goff, an NFL Player & daughter Lauren Goff, with her husband Jerry Leroy Goff, a former MLB player.

According to her LinkedIn profile, Nancy is a senior mortgage advisor at CrossCountry Mortgage, LLC (January 2020- Now).

The University of California, Berkley graduate formerly worked as a mortgage broker for 8 years at Terra Mortgage Banking (January 2012- 2020).

Brief About Nancy Goff’s Children

Both her children are now grown-ups. Nancy Goff’s daughter Lauren Goff, the eldest of two, was born on May 26 in the early 90s.

Lauren graduated from the University of California, Los Angeles, in 2014 with a Bachelor’s degree in International Development. Also she pursued her Master’s degree in Social Welfare from her alma mater.

Nancy’s daughter is married to Mason Butts; they exchanged vows on April 15, 2017.

Lauren Goff’s husband Mason is also an alumnus of UCLA. He graduated with a Bachelor’s degree in International Developmental Studies (2009-13) and a Master’s degree in Education (2014-16).

While she is an Executive Assistant to CFO аt Digilock, her husband Mason Butts is a professional development leader & web developer at Next Gen Personal Finance (Oct 2019- Now).

Before that, he was a chemistry/biology teacher at Los Angeles Unified School District (Aug 2015- July 2019).

Son Jared Is An NFL Player

An attendant of Marin Catholic High School, Jared Goff, enrolled at his parent’s alma mater UC Berkley (2013- 2015).

Throughout his collegiate football career, he set 26 college records, including passing yards (12,220), touchdown passes (96), and passing yards per game (329.7 ypg).

Then in the 2016 NFL Draft, he became the first overall pick of the LA Rams & signed with the team. As a rookie, he played in 7 games in the 2016 season.

In the next four seasons (2017 to 2020), Jared Goff was the Rams’ starting quarterback, totaling 1536 pass completions (2421 attempts), 107 passing touchdowns, and 55 interceptions thrown.

Ahead of the 2021 season, he was traded to Detroit Lions, where he finished with 19 touchdowns and eight interceptions for 3245 yards.

The 2022-23 regular season concluded with Jared’s 4,438 yards, 29 touchdowns, and seven interceptions starting in 17 games.

On June 16, 2022, Goff engaged his model girlfriend, Christen Harper. He confirmed the news via Instagram. The two began dating in 2019 but kept it under wraps for a while. 

An Upbringing To Make Her Son Kind & Calm

Being a football quarterback indeed means toughness & agility, but Nancy made sure that her son grew up strong yet kind.

During an interview with CBS’s KPIX 5 in the Goff family’s home in 2019, she shared a touching story about her son and Los Angeles offensive lineman Andrew Whitworth, one of Hared’s colleagues.

Jared Goff volunteered to bring Whitworth’s son a new pet fish because it appeared that he had just experienced the loss of his own.

She said:

“He brought up Michael, the young Whitworth child, from school. He took him with him to the fish shop. Pick up Goff and Gurley, two fresh fish. More than anything he will ever do, that kind of work means more to me than anything else.”

The NFL star’s parents said that Jared’s calm manner on the field comes from his upbringing as the son of an athlete and his mother’s character. She acknowledges that she might be partly responsible for his serenity.

Jared Goff’s Mother | Attends Sports Events Together

The mother-son duo often attend sports events together & there is barely any notable moment of her son that Nancy has missed.

 In the 2016 NFL Draft, Jared took his mom as his “date” for the red-carpet event.

When asked if Nancy would prefer Los Angeles to select her son to keep him in his home state, her answer was what you’d anticipate from a proud and encouraging mother.

Assuring Jared would be fantastic anywhere, she told Melissa Stark of NFL Network, “I just want him to go to a team that wants him and where he’ll be successful…”.

Jared Goff became the first-overall (top pick) choice of the Los Angeles Rams in the NFL Draft.

Other notable (top-5) NFL picks of 2016 were Carson Wentz (#2, QB), Joey Bosa (#3), Ezekiel Elliott (#4), and Jalen Ramsey (#5).

Nancy Goff always had faith in her son; his NFL prominence is proof of that faith & upbringing.

Although he struggled during his rookie season, Jared picked up the pace later & even led the Rams to Super Bowl III (but lost).

Fun Fact: When Jared Goff didn’t give attention to his family while at training camp, his mother would send him a picture of his golden retriever named Leo. After that, Goff would instantly text back.

Nancy Goff | Husband Is A Former MLB Player

Nancy Goff and her husband, Jerry Goff, have been married for decades, though they are yet to open up about their wedding details. The two met in high school in San Rafael High School.

Both even attended the University of California, Berkeley; while Nancy pursued her degree in International Economics, Jerry followed his baseball career.

As reported, Jerry Goff was drafted twice (Oakland Athletics in 1983 & the Yankees in 1984) as a high school student but opted to study at the University of California, Berkeley.

Later, the Seattle Mariners selected him in the third round (63rd overall) in the 1986 MLB Draft.

After struggling through the minor league system with the Mariners, he was traded to Montreal Expos in 1990 in exchange for Pat Pacillo.

On May 15, 1990, Jerry Goff made his MLB debut, which followed his first-ever major league hit on May 18, against San Francisco Giants pitcher Rick Reuschel.

Following that season, he returned to the minor league in 1991 before roster-calling in 1992. Throughout the next four years, Goff played as a backup catcher for Pittsburgh Pirates (1993-94) and Houston Astros (1995-96).

In 1997, Goff participated in one more season of professional baseball. Goff was chosen for the Texas-Louisiana League All-Star squad while playing for the independent Amarillo Dallas.

Later, Jerry retired from baseball.

What Is Jerry Goff Doing Now?

Nancy’s husband, Jerry (Jared’s father), is a firefighter.

But the baseball spirit wouldn’t leave him, so still a firefighter, he played semi-pro baseball with the Marin County, California-based Novato Knicks in 2001. 

Nancy Goff | Net Worth & Earnings

The exact figure of Nancy Goff’s net worth remains unknown. However, she makes a decent income as a senior mortgage advisor at CrossCountry Mortgage, LLC.

But it is undeniable that the proud NFL mom enjoys millionaire perks.

Her son Jared Goff owns a $50 million net worth, most of which came as lucrative salary from NFL contracts.

From his four-year $27.9 million contract (signed in 2016), he made $19 million just between 2016 and 2017. At that time, he received an annual salary of $7 million.

In 2018, his third season with the Rams, Goff led them to Super Bowl III (though not won), earning him a $22.783 million option for the 2020 season.

Subsequently, on September 3, 2019, Jared Goff signed a four-year, $134 million contract with the Rams. It included a $25 million signing bonus & $110 million guaranteed.

So, the NFL quarterback earned a total of $31 million in the 2020 season, including a $1 million salary, $21 million roster bonus & $9 million restructured bonus.

Upon trade to Detriot Lions, his salary of $20 million for the 2021-22 season was converted into a bonus. It earned him an additional  $5 million as the base salary from the Lions.

For the 2022-23 season, his annual earnings from NFL recorded $26.5 million ($10.65 million in salary & $15.5 million in bonus).

Moreover, Goff receives about $2 million yearly in endorsement fees from companies, including Red Bull, Pizza Hut, BMW, Bose, Banana Republic, and Nike.

In 2019, he introduced JG16, his apparel collection that included caps, hoodies, and T-shirts.

To provide one million meals for those in need during the coronavirus pandemic, the two-time Pro Bowl donated $250,000 to the L.A. Food Bank.

Jared purchased a home in a guarded neighborhood in Hidden Hills, California, for $4 million in May 2018. He sold his first house in the same month, a small house in Oak Park, California. The purchase price was $1.8 million.

Jared purchased a newly constructed home in Hermosa Beach, California, for close to $6 million in May 2020.
