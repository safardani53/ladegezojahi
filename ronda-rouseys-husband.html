Travis Browne | Early Life

Travis Browne (fully named Travis Kuualiialoha Browne) was born on July 17, 1982, in Honolulu, Hawaii, US. Well, he falls under the star sign of Cancer and is of mixed ethnicity. His father was of Native Hawaiian ancestry, while his mother was of European American ancestry.

Unfortunately, there are no thorough details regarding both of his parents. Yet, it is known that his father was an alcoholic and a gang member.

According to the sources, Travis often traveled around with his father in the car while his father carried a gun. 

Herewith, Travis Browne often dealt with problematic issues as a child. He would often see drugs lying around the house when his father’s friends would come over.

But as he recalls of now, he states his father to be good towards him as he would do everything to give him a good lifestyle. All in all, Travis lost his father at the tender age of ten. 

Following it, he moved to San Diego, California, with his mother. 

Education

Travis Browne attended the San Dieguito Academy High School. He played basketball during his high school tenure and even won the 2000 Coastal North League Player of the Year. 

As a high schooler, Travis got into numerous fights from frustrations. Even at one point, he nearly got into jail after breaking someone’s jaw. Later, he attended Palomar College for his higher education.
