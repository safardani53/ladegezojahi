What Team Does Pavard Play For? Career: Club & Country

To propel themself at the forefront of soccer, Benjamin joined Lille’s youth academy to hone his skills to land a professional contract.

The Swabians knew they had to let Benjamin go, and the money cashed in could be used for other reinforcements in the squad.

Notwithstanding, the French right-back shifts now and then to center-back during a personnel crisis, and this is where his versatility comes in handy.
